Tab 4 — Background Questions

All questions must be answered before continuing. "Yes" answers may require supplemental information.

1. Military service. Have you served in, or are you currently serving in, the U.S. military?
2. Legal actions. Are you currently a party to any administrative or legal action against the FAA?
3. Felony. Have you ever been convicted of, or pleaded guilty/no contest to, a felony?
4. Probation. Are you currently on probation, parole, or deferred adjudication?
5. Imprisonment. Have you been incarcerated in the past 7 years?
6. Certificate revocation. Has an airman certificate of yours ever been revoked, suspended, or surrendered?
7. English proficiency. Are you able to read, speak, write, and understand English?

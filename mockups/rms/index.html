N-Number Reservation

U.S. Registration Numbers (N-numbers) follow ICAO Annex 7 and 14 CFR Part 47. Up to 5 reservations per person. Reservations are valid for one year and may be renewed.

Format rules. N-numbers start with the letter N followed by 1–5 characters. Last 2 characters may be letters (A–Z, excluding I and O). Up to 5 numbers, or 3 numbers + 1 letter, or 2 numbers + 2 letters.

Aircraft Registration Renewal

The FAA Reauthorization Act of 2018 extended the renewal cycle from 3 years to 7 years. A Notice of Expiration is mailed approximately 6 months before the registration expires.

Notice letter security code required. Online renewal requires your N-number plus the random security code printed on the mailed Notice of Expiration. Lost the letter? Paper renewal (AC Form 8050-1B) with $5 fee is still available.

Step 1 — Identify Registration

Printed on the Notice of Expiration mailed 2025-12-25.

Step 2 — Confirm Information (no-change flow)

The online renewal path is an affirm-no-change flow. If any of the information below has changed, you must file a paper AC Form 8050-1B or a new AC Form 8050-1.

Privacy / Release Controls

Under the Privacy Act (5 U.S.C. § 552a) and SORN DOT/FAA 801, owners may request redaction of specific sensitive information from public display. To make a request:

  • File a Privacy Act amendment / redaction request through the FAA Privacy Office
  • Small UAS (Part 48) owners have automatic suppression of email and phone
  • FOIA path: FAA FOIA portal

Document Request

Request copies of aircraft registry records from the Image Management System (IMS). ~174M TIFF images are held; retention is permanent (NARA N1-237-04-03).

Fulfillment channels. Requests may be fulfilled by (a) aircraft record CD mailed to address on file (FY2017: 15,000 CDs mailed), (b) certified paper copies mailed, (c) uncertified download (after CARES cutover — in development). For real-time title search or ownership history, see the Public Documents Room (PDR) in Oklahoma City.

Request Details

Delivery

Fees summary. Complete record CD: $10. Total charged via Pay.gov. RMS stores only the transaction ID.

Alternative: Public Documents Room (PDR)

For real-time title search, ownership history, lien tracing, and same-day record access, the Public Documents Room at the Mike Monroney Aeronautical Center in Oklahoma City offers 47 workstations (42 held by permit-holder title/trust companies, 3 public). U.S. citizen public access requires 2 business days advance notice; international users 3 weeks. Permits are $3,441/year per workstation.

FAA plans to phase out the PDR once CARES delivers virtual real-time access.

Aircraft Dealer Certificate Application

An Aircraft Dealer Certificate under 14 CFR Part 47 Subpart C authorizes flight under the dealer's registration number without individual aircraft registration prior to sale. Dealer certificates are issued for a 1-year term and renewable.

Eligibility. Applicant must be a U.S. citizen (individual, partnership, corporation, or LLC), engaged in the business of manufacturing or selling aircraft, and have a permanent business address in the United States. A registered dealer may fly any aircraft in their possession under the dealer's N-number pending sale.
